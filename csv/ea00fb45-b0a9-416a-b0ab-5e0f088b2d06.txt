Financing receivables primarily consist of client loan and installment payment receivables (loans), investment in sales-type and direct financing leases, and commercial financing receivables. Client loan and installment payment receivables (loans) are provided primarily to clients to finance the purchase of hardware, software and services.
Payment terms on these financing arrangements are generally for terms up to seven years. Client loans and installment payment financing contracts are priced independently at competitive market rates.
