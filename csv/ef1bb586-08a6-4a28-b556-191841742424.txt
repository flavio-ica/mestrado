Netsmart Discontinued Operation
On December 31, 2018, we sold all of the Class A Common Units of Netsmart held by the Company in exchange for $566.6 million in cash plus a final settlement as determined following the closing.
Prior to the sale, Netsmart comprised a separate reportable segment, which due to its significance to our historical consolidated financial statements and results of operations, is reported as a discontinued operation as a result of the sale. Refer to Note 4, “Business Combinations and Other Investments” for additional information about this transaction.
